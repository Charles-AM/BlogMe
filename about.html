About

Regressed Ranker

Regressed Ranker is a personal blog for anime, manga, manhua, reviews, and recommendation lists.

The goal is simple: help readers find stories worth watching or reading. Posts are written in a casual voice, with honest opinions and clear reasons for each recommendation.

This site covers rankings, short reviews, watch notes, and list-style guides. We try to keep the writing useful, readable, and easy to enjoy.

Regressed Ranker is not connected to any anime studio, publisher, streaming service, or official franchise owner.
